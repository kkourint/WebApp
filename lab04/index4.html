What is Soccer?

Soccer, also know as football outside of the Unites States is a sport game played with a spherical ball between two teams of eleven players each. Today, soccer is played in approximately 200 countries and dependencies around the world. When i was growing, i loved soccer and spent most of my free time playing. My father even thought that i will become a professional player and kept encouraging me in practicing. But because of the uncertainty in the professional sport business, i decided to rather focus on my studies. Even though i did not play professional, i continue to play as part of my work out and also for fun. My favorite team is barcelona, a team i have been watching since my teenage years. On this website, i will be posting things about soccer, what i do and also news about my favorite team, Barcelona

About Barcelona!!

Barcelona Football club is a soccer team founded in November 1899 in the city of Barcelona, Spain. The founder, Hans Max Gamper-Haessig was a Swiss football pioneer, versatile athlete and club president. Beside barcelona, he also founded clubs in witzerland and Germany.
